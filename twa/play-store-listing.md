## Full Description

🏆 THE ULTIMATE FOOTBALL PREDICTION PLATFORM

Cup Clash lets you predict every match, compete with friends 
and colleagues in private groups, and follow the action with 
live scores — all for free.

--- PREDICT ON YOUR SCHEDULE ---

Join anytime and start predicting immediately. Whether you're 
in from day one or joining mid-tournament, Cup Clash adapts 
to you.

- Predict individual matches or the entire remaining schedule
- Tournament picks (Winner, Golden Boot, Golden Ball) locked 
  at a time chosen by your group admin
- Late-joining groups set their own prediction deadlines
- Every prediction counts — even partial participation earns points

--- HOW IT WORKS ---

Predict the score of every match before kick-off. Earn points 
for correct outcomes, and bonus points for nailing the exact 
scoreline. The closer your predictions, the higher you climb 
the leaderboard.

Scoring is simple and fair:
- Exact scoreline → full points
- Correct winner/draw → partial points  
- Knockout stage → bonus points per correct advancement
- Tournament picks → big points for long-term predictions

--- COMPETE IN PRIVATE GROUPS ---

Create a private group and invite friends, family, or 
workmates with a single link or passkey. Your personal 
leaderboard updates live after every match.

Group types:
- Friend Circle — casual group for friends and family
- Corporate — company-wide competition with custom prizes
- Family & School — fun for all ages, no money involved

The admin controls everything — scoring rules, payout splits, 
bonus questions, member management, and group settings.

--- FOLLOW EVERY MATCH ---

- Live scores updated automatically after every match
- Goal notifications and rank-change alerts via Telegram
- Full tournament bracket from Group Stage to the Final
- Group standings table for all teams
- Top Scorers and Assisters leaderboard
- Match results with prediction breakdown per member

--- KEY FEATURES ---

✓ Predict all matches across supported tournaments
✓ Private groups with invite links
✓ Live leaderboard updated after every match
✓ Tournament picks: Winner, Golden Boot, Golden Ball & more
✓ Custom bonus questions set by group admin
✓ Progressive scoring — later rounds worth more points
✓ Group chat with GIF support
✓ Push notifications for goals and rank changes
✓ Available in 8 languages: English, French, Spanish,
  Portuguese, German, Dutch, Hebrew & Arabic
✓ Free to use — optional ad-free upgrade available

--- BUILT FOR ALL FOOTBALL ---

Cup Clash is designed for every major football competition:

International Tournaments:
World Cup, European Championship, Copa América, 
African Cup of Nations, Asian Cup and more

Club Competitions:
UEFA Champions League, Europa League, Premier League, 
La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, MLS and more

Currently featuring FIFA World Cup 2026.
More tournaments and leagues coming soon.

--- FREE & FAIR ---

Cup Clash is completely free to play. No paywalls on 
predictions, no locked features for your group. An optional

Football brings people together. Cup Clash makes it
