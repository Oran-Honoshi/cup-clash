What's handled for you

Why these won't break

⌀
No white edges

The green pitch bleeds fully to all four corners. When iOS/Android round the icon, there's never a pale gap — the crop reveals more green, not white.

80
Maskable safe-zone

On the maskable file the trophy lives inside the inner 80% circle Android guarantees, so circle, squircle, teardrop and rounded-square masks all keep the cup intact.

16
Legible when tiny

The favicon zooms to the cup bowl instead of shrinking the whole scene, so the gold still reads as a trophy at 16px in a crowded tab strip.

✓
Right file, right slot

Standard names and sizes (apple-touch-icon, maskable, og-image) map 1:1 to what the manifest and meta tags expect.

★
Theme-matched chrome

Manifest theme_color / background_color sampled from the artwork (#0a1b0e) so the splash and status bar blend in.

⤓
